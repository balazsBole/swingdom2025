Swing in Utrecht is responsible for spreading the swing virus in the Utrecht region in the form of lessons, events and parties. In 2007 it was founded by Arjan de Boer, Annette Duivenvoorde and Jojanneke Heidema. Since then the lindy hop scene in Utrecht has been growing enormously and more and more lessons, parties, workshops are added and lots of new lindy hoppers are joining the fun. The foundation could not exist without the enthusiasm and energy of countless volunteers that have caught the virus.
