About SwingDom

We will soon welcome you to our annual Lindy Hop festival, SwingDom. SwingDom is an event organised by the Lindy Hop community, Swing In Utrecht. Starting out as a small local event SwingDom has grown out to become the biggest international swing festival in the Netherlands!

Utrecht

This medieval city is known for its canals and an overwhelming amount of bicycles. The greatest pride of all Utrechters is the Dom tower. Naturally the Dom tower is also represented in the name of SwingDom

Swing in Utrecht

We are an inclusive, international community of people that share a passion for swing dancing. Together we dance Lindy hop, Charleston, Solo jazz, Blues, Shag and Balboa to Jazz and related music styles
