About SwingDom

We will soon welcome you to our annual Lindy Hop festival, SwingDom. SwingDom is an event organised by the Lindy Hop community, Swing In Utrecht. Starting out as a small local event SwingDom has grown out to become the biggest international swing festival in the Netherlands!

The Big Five

We are very happy to have The Big Five @thebigfivejazzberlin share their music.
Inspired by the wild hot sounds of 1930s/40s Harlem and Chicago jazz, The Big Five have established themselves as Berlin’s most authentic swing-era combo. Combining a driving rhythm section, a fiery horn-led frontline and dynamic song-arrangements, this international band makes a deep-dive into the lesser-known repertoire of the Swing Golden Age - as well as a strong emphasis on original compositions.
The Big Five are veterans of Berlin’s famed Lindy-hop and Balboa dance-halls and know how to turn a mild-mannered dance party into a raging frenzy of swing-outs.

Roaring Cats

We are very happy to welcome back The Roaring Cats(@roaring.cats), and have them share their wonderful music at our SwingDom 2025 Sunday Party.
The Roaring Cats will take us right back to the ’20s and ‘30s of New Orleans with their repertoire ranging from smooth slow-passed songs to swinging high tempo jazz. Get ready to dance the night away!
